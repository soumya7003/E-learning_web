Frequently Asked Questions

What is Galayr Academy?

Galayr Academy is your tech learning partner. We offer job-focused, practical courses designed for both beginners and professionals. Our live classes help build strong foundations in programming, logic, and modern technologies such as web development, SQL, Python, Java, and more.

Who is Galayr Academy for?

Absolute beginners who have little or no coding experience. Students or aspiring developers who prefer learning in their own language to better understand concepts. People who want a structured, step-by-step course with guidance, live sessions, and examples.

What is the teaching style / format at Galayr Academy?

Live classes with real-time interaction, so students can ask questions and get help. Hands-on practice: assignments, coding exercises, projects to apply what has been taught.

What are some benefits / value-adds of Galayr Academy?

Learning in regional / native language helps clarity and understanding. Affordable pricing and practical value for beginners. Preparation for real-world jobs, technical interviews; logic building helps in competitive scenarios.
